RNCP 37394 · The French Context
Not just anchored — adaptably relevant
France's certification framework defines both blocs de compétences and éléments de preuve needed to validate them. The competency taxonomy is externally validated by the state. The AI finds proof that already meets this bar.
Key design principle: separate the evidence layer from the mapping layer. Raw traces are permanent. RNCP labels are one interpretation — re-mappable as definitions evolve. When industry signals competencies beyond current RNCP scope, the framework captures them as emerging signals, potentially informing future national certification revision.
DC Discussion · Seeking Your Expertise
Q1 · Data Sparsity
Rubric evaluations are brief compared to typical NLP datasets. How can we ensure reliable AI competency mapping without massive domain-specific training data?
Q2 · HITL Scalability
Asking educators to validate AI outputs per student risks overload. What are practical strategies to keep human review light and manageable at scale?
Q3 · Profile Validity
Students, educators, and employers each expect different things from a competency profile. Whose view should anchor validity checks — self-view, educator judgment, or employer needs?
