About Me

I'm Sonita, a PhD student in Data Science & AI for Education, passionate about using technology to transform how people learn.

My path began in Cambodia as a software developer and teaching assistant, which gave me a deep appreciation for both building systems and nurturing students.

Awarded a scholarship to pursue my Master's degree in France, I returned to Cambodia to take on leadership roles at CADT — first as Program Coordinator, then as Head of the Department of Computer Science. Once again recognised with a scholarship, I am now pursuing my PhD in France.

Today, my research sits at the intersection of artificial intelligence and education, asking: how can data science make learning more equitable and effective?
